Terms and Conditions for School Enrollment:

1. The child's age must not be less than three years and two months at the time of enrollment.

2. A copy of the child's birth certificate and ID card must be provided.

3. Three colored passport photos must be provided.

4. A copy of the parent's ID card (father or mother).

5. The academic year begins in September and ends in May.

6. Official school hours begin at 8:00 AM and end at 11:30 AM. Late arrival in the morning or leaving before the end of official hours must be accompanied by a medical excuse accepted by the school administration.

7.The school has a private uniform. Payment is made through the guardian. The student must attend wearing clean, proper uniform.

Textbooks:

8. The total transportation and books fees are paid in full at the beginning of the academic year. The tuition fees can be divided into installments according to an agreement with the school administration.

9. Installments are paid at the beginning of the month, not at the end. The paid installment is non-refundable after five days from payment. Delays in payments must be arranged with school administration during a specified period.

10. The academic year consists of nine months, beginning in September and ending in May. The school is not responsible for student absence at the beginning of the year. Students must commit to paying the full nine months' fees.

11. If the guardian wishes to withdraw the student and not complete their studies, they must pay the full fees for the period until the end of the academic semester in which they withdraw.

12. The mid-year holiday will be according to the Ministry of Education guidelines and its value is included in the school fees.

13. School fees for the academic year must be completed before March 1st.

Textbooks:

14. Students must maintain textbooks in good condition and keep them clean. 15. The school is not responsible for compensating for lost or damaged textbooks after receiving them from the school administration.

16. The guardian is responsible for providing the school bag, school supplies, and any other necessities the student needs.

17. The guardian must write the student's name on all school supplies, food containers, and drinking equipment to avoid loss or conflict with other children due to similarity with their belongings.

Transportation Usage:

18. The use of the transportation means is completely voluntary. The school administration will inform the parent/guardian of the pick-up location at the appropriate time. The school is not responsible for transporting the student in case of delay.

19. In the case of residents in areas where bus access is difficult, the parent/guardian must bring the student to a place where the bus can stop and wait for them upon their return.

20. In case the student's residence location changes during the academic year, the school is not responsible for transporting them to the new location, especially if the new location is outside the bus route.



I, the undersigned, parent/guardian of the student, testify that I have reviewed the terms and conditions of joining the school and that I will act according to what is stated therein.
