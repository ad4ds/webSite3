A Warm Welcome to Our School Family

Dear Esteemed Parents and Guardians,

It is with great pleasure and honor that I welcome you to Dar Al-Hidaya School, where we have been committed to excellence in Islamic education for over two decades. Since our founding in 2000, we have dedicated ourselves to nurturing young minds while instilling the sacred values of Islam, fostering patriotism, and building strong character foundations.

At Dar Al-Hidaya, we believe that each child is a precious trust (Amanah) placed in our care. Our distinguished faculty of seven experienced educators work tirelessly across our nine well-equipped classrooms to provide your children with a comprehensive education that balances academic excellence with spiritual growth. Our curriculum seamlessly integrates Quranic memorization, Arabic literacy, English proficiency, mathematics, and science within an Islamic framework that prepares our students for both worldly success and eternal prosperity.

We invite you to join our school family, where tradition meets excellence, and where your child's educational journey will be guided by Islamic principles and academic rigor.

Barakallahu feeki,
Ms. Tahani Saif Al-Amri
